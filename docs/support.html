Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your financial data stays on your device. Mooney never uploads your transactions, accounts, or balances to any server.

How do I back up my data?

Use the export feature in Settings to save your data as a file. You can then store it in your preferred cloud storage or send it via email.

Can I use Mooney on multiple devices?

Mooney currently stores data locally on each device. Cloud sync between devices is not yet available.

How do I restore a purchase?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription status will be synced from the App Store or Google Play.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling the app removes all data from your device. You can also clear app data from your device settings.

Which currencies are supported?

Mooney supports 150+ currencies with live exchange rates. You can set your base currency in Settings and track accounts in any currency.
