Mooney is a beautifully simple money tracker for people who think in more than one currency.

Track expenses across PLN, EUR, USD and 30+ other currencies. See your real net worth at a glance. Hit your savings goals faster.

▸ MULTICURRENCY BY DESIGN
Add accounts in any currency. Mooney converts everything to your base currency using live European Central Bank rates — no more spreadsheets, no more guessing.

▸ ACCOUNTS, NOT JUST WALLETS
Cash, debit, credit, savings — model every account you use. Move money between them with a single transfer, no double bookkeeping.

▸ TRANSACTIONS THAT ACTUALLY MAKE SENSE
Hand-picked categories. Recurring transactions for rent and subscriptions. Quick-add from the home screen. Filter and search by anything.

▸ GOALS YOU'LL ACTUALLY REACH
Set savings goals with deadlines. See live progress as you save. Group related goals — "Emergency fund + house deposit + vacation" — and watch them advance together.

▸ PRIVACY-FIRST
All your financial data stays on your device. We never see your transactions, balances, or accounts. No login. No account creation. No ads in your data.

▸ DESIGNED TO FEEL GOOD
Polished dark mode. Smooth animations. Designed by an indie developer who actually uses this app every day.
